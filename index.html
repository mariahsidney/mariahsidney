With Jay

This fan project is dedicated to Jay from ENHYPEN and serves as a heartfelt tribute from his fans, known as ENGENEs. The website features a collection of personal letters from fans expressing their support and admiration for Jay, along with beautiful fan arts created by the community. Additionally, the project includes a surprise video compilation that showcases messages and creativity from ENGENEs, celebrating Jay's journey and impact as an artist. This project not only highlights the love and dedication of fans but also aims to create a warm and encouraging space for everyone who supports Jay.

Technologies Used: Wix

View Project

Learning Outcomes: Through this fan project, I enhanced my web design skills, learned the importance of community engagement, improved content organization, developed collaboration abilities, and gained technical proficiency using Wix.
